Pro Tips to Dominate CODM 🎯

🎯 Use Cover Always

Never stay in the open. Move between walls and objects to avoid getting eliminated quickly.

🔫 Control Your Recoil

Drag your aim slightly downward while shooting to stay accurate and hit more shots.

👂 Listen to Footsteps

Use headphones to detect enemies nearby and react faster.

⚡ Improve Movement

Slide, jump, and strafe during fights to make yourself harder to hit.

🧠 Learn the Maps

Knowing spawn points and hiding spots gives you a huge advantage.

🎯 Pre-Aim Corners

Always aim before turning corners to react faster and win gunfights.

⚡ Use Slide Cancel

Slide and quickly aim to reset movement and confuse enemies.

🔄 Reload Smartly

Don’t reload in the open. Always find cover before reloading.

🧭 Watch Mini Map

Check the mini map often to track enemy positions and teammate deaths.

💣 Use Scorestreaks Wisely

Save powerful scorestreaks for important moments, not randomly.

🎧 Adjust Sensitivity

Find the right sensitivity settings to improve your aim and control.

🔥 Practice Aim Daily

Spend time in practice mode to improve accuracy and reaction time.

🛡️ Don’t Rush Blindly

Think before pushing enemies. Rushing without info gets you killed.
